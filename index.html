The Beginner's Guide to Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

A free, 98-chapter interactive book that starts from zero prerequisites — no coding, math, or ML background assumed — and builds up to explaining and building real systems: YOLO, SuperPoint, SuperGlue, large language models, vision-language models, and diffusion models. Every chapter pairs a plain-language explanation with a real, verifiable interactive demo and a short quiz, so you can check you actually understood it — for anyone who wants to go from complete beginner to having a genuinely solid foundation in AI/ML.
